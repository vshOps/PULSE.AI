# PULSE — Predictive Unified Lifecycle System for Everyone

> "PULSE doesn't make doctor visits smarter. It makes the time between visits matter."

---

## What is PULSE?

PULSE is an AI-powered Care Operating System that keeps the doctor-patient relationship alive 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — not just during the 10-minute consultation.

Every existing healthcare app digitizes the clinic visit. PULSE eliminates the gap between visits.

India has 77 million diabetic patients and only 1 doctor for every 1456 patients. A typical consultation lasts 8 to 10 minutes. What happens in the 3 months between visits? Nobody knows. Patients forget medicine. Stress builds up. Warning signs appear but nobody catches them. Preventable emergencies happen every day.

PULSE fills that silence.

---

## The Core Problem

Healthcare fails not inside the clinic — it fails in the silence between visits.

- Patient sees doctor for 10 minutes every 3 months
- Leaves with a prescription they half understand.
- No follow-up system, no monitoring, no connection.
- Different doctors, pharmacists, specialists never communicate
- Family caregiver at home has no information or guidance
- Preventable emergencies happen because nobody connected the dots

## The Solution — 5 Core Features

### 1. The Mirror Score
A single health score from 0 to 100 — like a health credit score — calculated from 5 dimensions:
- **Physical** — vitals, labs, wearable data
- **Mental** — stress, mood, sleep quality
- **Lifestyle** — diet, exercise, habits
- **Adherence** — medication pickups, follow-up attendance
- **Social** — family support, isolation risk

The score updates in real time. Patients understand it. Doctors act on it instantly.

### 2. Causal Chain Detection
Every app shows WHAT is wrong. PULSE shows WHY.

Example:PULSE connects all data signals into a causal story and shows the doctor the root cause — not just the symptom. Treating the root cause instead of the symptom is what prevents emergencies.

### 3. The Care Web
A patient's health involves their GP, specialist, pharmacist, nutritionist, physiotherapist, and family caregiver. Today they all work in silos. PULSE creates one shared workspace where every care team member sees the same real-time picture, communicates, assigns tasks, and tracks outcomes together. The patient sits at the center — as an active participant, not a passive receiver.

### 4. Emotion-Aware Ambient Scribe
During consultations PULSE listens with full patient consent and:
- Auto-generates clinical SOAP notes in real time
- Detects patient emotional state — fear, confusion, denial, anxiety
- Quietly alerts the doctor so they can adjust how they communicate
- After the visit, translates the care plan into the patient's regional language and sends it via WhatsApp

### 5. Predictive Risk Radar
PULSE predicts upcoming health events weeks before they happen. For each patient it shows:
- The specific risk with percentage likelihood
- The timeframe — for example 67% chance of requiring insulin within 4 months
- The data signals driving that prediction
- The recommended preventive action
